## OVERVIEW:
This database system appears to be designed for managing Randstad's recruitment operations and client relationships. It comprises four main components: `Dixi2`, which functions as a detailed repository for job applicant information, capturing everything from contact details and preferred roles to forklift licenses and background check comfort; `RDMasterlist`, serving as a comprehensive master list for corporate clients, categorizing them by industry, client group, and assigning key account personnel; `audit_log`, which diligently records system activities, specifically user-initiated data modifications; and `users`, which manages user authentication and roles within the system. Together, these tables enable Randstad to effectively track potential candidates, manage its corporate client portfolio, and maintain an auditable record of system changes, supporting their staffing and recruitment services.
